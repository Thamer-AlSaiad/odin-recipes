Peaches and Cream Cocktail

This fresh peaches and cream cocktail screams summer. Peaches, peach schnapps, and vodka combine in this simple yet tasty frozen drink.

Peaches and Cream Cocktail Ingredients

  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 1/2 cups peeled and chopped fresh peaches
  • 1/4 cup peach schnapps
  • 1/4 cup whipped vodka
  • frozen whipped topping (such as Cool WhipÂ®), thawed, for rim
  • peach slice for garnish
    Directions
  1. step 1

    2. Place ice in a blender and crush for 15 to 20 seconds. Add fresh peaches, peach schnapps, and whipped vodka. Blend until smooth.

  2. step 2

    3. Rim glass with frozen whipped topping. Pour cocktail into glass and serve garnished with peach slice.
